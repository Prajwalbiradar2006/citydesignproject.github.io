Information Technology (IT Sector)

SDG Target 11.3 – Sustainable Urban Planning
  • Smart‑city solutions including traffic management, digital mapping, and waste monitoring systems.
  • Knowledge‑based employment supporting sustainable urban growth.
  • Use of GIS, digital twins, and automation to support efficient infrastructure.

Hotel Management

SDG Target 11.3 – Sustainable Urban Development
  • Provides hygienic and safe accommodation within structured city zones.
  • Reduces congestion by being placed near commercial hubs and transport routes.
  • Adopts eco‑friendly practices such as water saving and waste reduction.

Bar Management

SDG Target 11.7 – Safe & Inclusive Spaces
  • Promotes regulated and safe nightlife zones.
  • Reduces illegal or unsafe drinking spots.
  • Implements waste segregation and noise‑control measures.

Post Office Management

SDG Target 11.1 – Access to Basic Services
  • Essential communication and parcel services for homes and businesses.
  • Supports digital and financial inclusion of citizens.
  • Ensures organized parcel logistics and secure delivery networks.

Petrol Pump Management

SDG Target 11.6 – Lower Environmental Impact
  • Provides safe, regulated fuel distribution.
  • Encourages green mobility using EV charging & CNG services.
  • Uses vapour‑recovery and pollution control systems.

Supermarket Management

SDG Target 11.1 – Essential Urban Services
  • Provides affordable essentials in hygienic, structured spaces.
  • Reduces street congestion by centralising retail.
  • Uses recyclable packaging and waste management systems.

Vehicle Showroom Management

SDG Target 11.2 – Sustainable Transport
  • Promotes adoption of electric and low‑emission vehicles.
  • Provides regulated service centres reducing pollution from informal workshops.
  • Supports EV charging and clean mobility awareness.

Hospital Management

SDG Target 11.3 – Accessible Services
  • Provides essential healthcare ensuring urban well‑being.
  • Improves emergency response & disaster readiness.
  • Ensures safe biomedical waste disposal.

School Management

SDG Target 11.7 – Safe Public Spaces
  • Provides accessible education within planned localities.
  • Creates safe learning and recreational spaces.
  • Maintains playgrounds used as community spaces.

Market (Bazaar) Management

SDG Target 11.6 – Clean Urban Environment
  • Organises business into structured, clean, less‑congested zones.
  • Supports local vendors in hygienic retail spaces.
  • Adopts proper waste disposal, sanitation & drainage systems.
