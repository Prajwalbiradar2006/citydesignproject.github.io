Urban Business Ideas – Set 5

10 New Smart, Sustainable City Business Solutions

Smart Air Quality Monitoring & Pollution Control Hub

SDG 11.6 – Reduce Urban Environmental Impact
  • Installs real-time pollution sensors across the city.
  • Provides live AQI alerts, traffic emission tracking, and health warnings.
  • Helps identify pollution hotspots and supports environmental actions.

Robotics-Based City Cleaning Unit

SDG 11.5 – Improve Urban Safety & Hygiene
  • Robotic street sweepers, drain cleaners, and lake-cleaning bots.
  • Reduces manual scavenging and ensures 24/7 cleanliness.
  • Enhances public hygiene and reduces labour risks.

E-Library & Digital Knowledge Access Center

SDG 11.4 – Strengthen Cultural & Educational Access
  • Provides online books, journals, courses, and newspapers.
  • Promotes digital literacy and modern learning methods.
  • Reduces the cost of education and increases accessibility.

Shared Micro-Mobility Hub (E-Cycles & E-Scooters)

SDG 11.2 – Sustainable Mobility
  • Electric cycle and scooter rental stations across the city.
  • Reduces traffic congestion and air pollution.
  • Supports short-distance eco-friendly travel.

Rainwater Recharge & Borewell Health Service

SDG 6.6 – Protect Water Resources
  • Installs recharge pits to improve groundwater levels.
  • Provides borewell cleaning and water-level monitoring.
  • Solves water scarcity and supports water sustainability.

Food Waste Composting & Bio-Fertilizer Production Center

SDG 12.3 – Reduce Food Waste
  • Collects food waste from homes, hotels, and markets.
  • Produces organic compost and natural fertilizers.
  • Supports urban farming and reduces landfill waste.

Smart Emergency Alert & Disaster Response System

SDG 11.5 – Disaster Risk Reduction
  • IoT-based flood, fire, and earthquake early-warning sensors.
  • Emergency sirens, SMS alerts, and real-time data monitoring.
  • Improves disaster preparedness and saves lives.

Digital Skill Development & Remote Work Center

SDG 8.3 – Promote Youth Employment
  • Training in AI, robotics, coding, cybersecurity, and digital marketing.
  • Co-working spaces for freelancers and remote workers.
  • Boosts digital economy and employment opportunities.

Green Roofing & Solar Terrace Farming Services

SDG 11.7 – Greener Urban Spaces
  • Builds rooftop gardens and terrace farms.
  • Installs solar panels to reduce electricity bills.
  • Improves air quality and lowers urban heat.

Smart Pet Adoption & Lost-Animal Tracking Center

SDG 11.7 – Safe Community Spaces
  • Manages GPS pet tags and lost-animal recovery system.
  • Runs a modern adoption center with veterinary services.
  • Reduces stray animal population and improves safety.
