Additional Urban Business Ideas — SDG 11 Contributions

10 new business concepts for a sustainable city — Prajwal Biradar

Smart Parking & Traffic Automation System

SDG 11.2 – Sustainable Transport & Mobility
Contribution
  • Real-time parking availability and dynamic pricing to reduce time spent searching for parking (cut idling & emissions).
  • Traffic-signal coordination and adaptive routing to reduce congestion on main corridors.
  • Integrates with public transport apps to encourage multi-modal travel.
Sustainable Practices
  • Solar-powered parking sensors and low-energy signage.
  • Priority for EV & carpool vehicles to promote cleaner transport.
  • Data-driven planning to identify infrastructure improvements that reduce emissions.

Urban Farming & Hydroponics Center

SDG 11.A – Urban–Rural Economic Links
Contribution
  • Fresh vegetables and herbs grown locally, reducing food miles and supply-chain vulnerability.
  • Creates jobs in urban agriculture, training, and micro-enterprise (farm-to-market stalls).
  • Educational demonstration farms for schools and communities.
Sustainable Practices
  • Soilless hydroponic systems that use up to 90% less water than open-field farming.
  • LED horticultural lighting on a timed, energy-efficient schedule powered by rooftop solar.
  • Composting of plant waste and local reuse of effluents after safe treatment.

Public EV Charging & Battery Swap Station

SDG 11.6 & 11.2 – Clean Transport
Contribution
  • Enables wider EV adoption by reducing range anxiety with fast chargers and battery-swap options.
  • Supports commercial fleets (taxis, delivery) to electrify, lowering urban emissions.
  • Generates maintenance & technician jobs in green mobility services.
Sustainable Practices
  • Chargers paired with local solar+storage to lower grid peaks and CO₂ footprint.
  • Recycling program for used batteries and safe disposal protocols.
  • Smart scheduling to reduce idle charging and grid load.

Smart Street Lighting & Electrical Maintenance Unit

SDG 11.6 – Energy Efficiency
Contribution
  • Installs LED streetlights with motion sensors and dimming to reduce nighttime energy use.
  • Provides quick fault detection & remote maintenance to keep public spaces safe and lit.
  • Offers retrofitting services to upgrade old infrastructure across neighborhoods.
Sustainable Practices
  • Solar + battery micro-stations for remote / park lighting.
  • Use of long-life LED fixtures and recyclable components.
  • Data monitoring to optimize energy schedules by usage patterns.

Digital Payment & FinTech Service Center

SDG 11.1 & 11.A – Inclusive Services
Contribution
  • Enables cashless payments across markets, transit, utilities, and vendors for convenience and transparency.
  • Provides micro-loans and digital financial literacy programs to small entrepreneurs.
  • Integrates municipal payments (property tax, water bills) into one app for citizens.
Sustainable Practices
  • Low-energy server architecture and cloud-native services to reduce IT energy footprint.
  • Paperless billing & e-receipts to lower paper waste.
  • Community kiosks for digital inclusion where smartphone access is limited.

Smart Drainage & Rainwater Harvesting System

SDG 11.3 & 11.6 – Resilient Infrastructure
Contribution
  • Prevents urban flooding with real-time flow sensors and smart sluice control.
  • Collects rainwater for reuse in irrigation, toilets, and cooling systems.
  • Reduces stormwater pollution by adding bio-filtration nodes before discharge.
Sustainable Practices
  • Permeable pavements and infiltration trenches to increase groundwater recharge.
  • Solar-powered sensor networks and low-energy valves.
  • Modular harvesting tanks that can be retrofitted to existing buildings.

Public Transport Card & Ticketing System

SDG 11.2 – Accessible Transport
Contribution
  • Single reloadable card/app works across buses, shuttles, metro, and parking for seamless travel.
  • Offers concession fares for students, seniors, and low-income riders to improve equity.
  • Gathers anonymized travel data to improve route planning and frequency.
Sustainable Practices
  • Contactless payments reduce paper ticket waste.
  • Incentives for off-peak travel to smooth demand and energy use.
  • Low-power NFC cards and durable materials for long service life.

Smart Health Monitoring & Telemedicine Hub

SDG 11.3 – Inclusive Urban Services
Contribution
  • Remote consultations, digital prescriptions, and home-monitoring for chronic patients.
  • Reduces pressure on hospitals by triaging non-emergency care online.
  • Maintains digital health records that travel with the patient across clinics.
Sustainable Practices
  • Low-bandwidth telemedicine options for underserved areas.
  • Use of energy-efficient servers and secure cloud storage.
  • Community health-monitor kiosks to reduce unnecessary travel.

Cultural & Event Management Center

SDG 11.7 – Inclusive & Safe Public Spaces
Contribution
  • Organizes festivals, markets, workshops and cultural events to strengthen social cohesion.
  • Provides income opportunities to local artisans and performers.
  • Manages public halls for community use, reducing private rentals and fragmentation.
Sustainable Practices
  • Zero-waste event planning with reusable staging and compostable materials.
  • Local sourcing for food and materials to reduce transport emissions.
  • Event scheduling to minimize noise and disturbance to residential areas.

City Security & Surveillance Network

SDG 11.7 – Safe Cities
Contribution
  • Monitors public spaces for safety incidents, coordinates with emergency services, and supports crime prevention.
  • Provides data for urban planners to identify risky locations and improve design.
  • Offers neighborhood watch integrations and public SOS buttons for quick assistance.
Sustainable Practices
  • Edge-compute CCTV to reduce central server load and energy consumption.
  • Solar-powered camera poles in parks and remote areas.
  • Privacy-by-design policies: anonymized data, strict retention policies, and community oversight.
